TaskCam

TaskCam is a task-management app designed to increase productivity by combining social accountability and real-time progress tracking. Users can create a to-do list, complete tasks by taking photos, and share them with friends for accountability. We presented TaskCam at a case competition at Indiana University, designing the MVP using React Native for the front end and Node.js for the backend.

  • Increases accountability and motivation by sharing task progress with friends
  • Tracks productivity with detailed stats and progress reports
  • Uses a photo-based task completion system to enhance engagement

The app was inspired by a commitment to self-improvement and helps users turn productivity into a habit.
